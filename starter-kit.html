Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Starter Kit?

The Starter Kit includes a Shelva Hub, sample labels, mounting accessories, and everything you need to test Shelva with real hardware in your store.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping can take 1-6 weeks depending on your location and current stock availability. We'll provide tracking information once your order ships.

How long does starter kit setup take?

Most stores complete starter kit setup in under 10 minutes. Just plug in the Hub, open the Shelva app in Shopify, and pair your labels to products.

What's your return policy for starter kits?

Starter kits are fully refundable on return. Return your starter kit in good condition within 30 days for a full refund — fast and hassle-free.

Can I test the starter kit before committing to a full order?

Yes! The starter kit is designed specifically for hands-on testing. You can test Shelva with real hardware in your store, see live updates, and experience the full system before placing a larger order.

What support is included with the starter kit?

Our team provides full onboarding and setup support for your starter kit. We'll help you get everything connected and working, and answer any questions you have during your testing period.
