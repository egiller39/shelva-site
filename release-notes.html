Instant Product Syncing

You can now choose between scheduled syncing or our new instant sync option. When instant sync is enabled, any price or product change in Shopify updates your labels automatically in real time. This keeps your labels accurate without manual action.

For instant sync mode, we've also introduced a new tracking system that shows every product update Shelva receives from Shopify. This gives you a clear view of what changed, when it changed, and whether the update reached your labels.

Improved Location Management

Locations are now archived instead of deleted. Archived locations stay out of your way in the app but remain available if you ever need them again. This helps keep your store list clean without losing any history.

New Font Option

We've added Figtree as a new font option in the label design page. Merchants can now select Figtree from the font list when customizing their shelf labels for a fresh, modern look.
